Usage

Keep it simple. The brand works best when nothing fights for attention.

Do

Use with restraint

  • Use the SVG logos provided here
  • Keep clear space around the mark
  • Write "Shibumi Stack" with both words capitalized
  • Use solid light or dark backgrounds
Avoid

Do not overwork it

  • Do not alter, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Do not place it on busy backgrounds
  • Do not write "ShibumiStack" or "SHIBUMI STACK"
  • Do not imply endorsement
