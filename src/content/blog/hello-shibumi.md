# Hello, Shibumi

This is the first post on the Shibumi Stack blog.

The stack is built around a simple idea: **own your source**. No hidden framework runtime, no magic you can't trace. Just Bun, Hono, and plain files you can read and modify.

We're starting with this site — dogfooding the stack before we ship the scaffolder. Every page has a Markdown source. Every route is explicit. Every decision is documented.
